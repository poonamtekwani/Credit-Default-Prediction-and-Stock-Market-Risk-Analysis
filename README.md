# 📊 Financial Risk Analysis & Credit Default Prediction

🔹 Machine Learning | Finance Analytics | Risk Modeling

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing financial data of companies to assess risk and predict potential defaults using machine learning techniques.

A predictive model was developed to classify whether a company is likely to become a **defaulter based on its financial performance and balance sheet indicators**.

Additionally, market risk analysis was performed using statistical methods to evaluate stock volatility and investment risk.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Investors and financial institutions need to identify financially unstable companies to minimize risk.

This project aims to:

- Predict company default risk using financial metrics  
- Analyze financial health through liquidity, profitability, and leverage ratios  
- Evaluate market risk using stock return volatility  

---

## 📂 Dataset

- 4256 companies  
- 51 financial features  
- Includes:
  - Net worth, total assets, income, expenses  
  - Profitability metrics (PBDITA, PBT, PAT)  
  - Liquidity & leverage ratios  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### Part A: Credit Risk Prediction
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on financial variables  
- Identified skewness and presence of outliers  
- Correlation analysis revealed strong relationships among profit metrics  
- Built classification models to predict defaulters  

### Part B: Market Risk Analysis
- Analyzed stock return distributions  
- Used mean and standard deviation to measure volatility  
- Assessed portfolio risk and variability  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Financial data is highly skewed, indicating variability across companies  
- Profit-related variables show strong correlation  
- High debt and low liquidity increase default probability  
- Stock volatility plays a key role in investment risk  

---

## 💼 Business Impact

- Enables early identification of high-risk companies  
- Supports better credit and investment decisions  
- Helps in managing financial and market risk effectively
